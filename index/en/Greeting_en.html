Greeting

We have been striving to create only the best moments with innovative technology that cannot be imitated in the Korean ornamental fish industry.

We will make more efforts in the future to grow into a global company!

Hello, customers.

For the past 15 years, QUAKOREA has been maintaining its brand name with a sense of mission to become the foundation for the development of the aquarium industry as a developer and manufacturer of freshwater/saltwater ornamental fish products.

Cua Korea, which started as a very small acrylic handmade product workshop, has become a leading player in handmade products for Korean aquariums with preemptive technology and differentiated items thanks to the generous love and support of customers who trust our products.

Now you are ready to take a further leap forward by building a mass production system.

QUAKOREA is continuing its unstoppable growth based on its corporate philosophy of passion not to be afraid of ‘first time’ and a spirit of challenge that is not afraid of ‘failure’.

Based on QUA's unique strengths and experiences accumulated over the past 15 years, we will further develop into a "first base" for the global aquarium industry.

Thank you.
